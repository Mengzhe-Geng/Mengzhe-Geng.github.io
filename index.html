My name is GENG 耿 /kə̌ŋ/, Mengzhe 梦哲 /mə̀ŋ ʈʂɤ́/ (click for pronunciation). I am currently a PhD student in Cognitive Science and Language at the Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona.

I work on morphology, syntax, semantics and their interfaces, with a particular focus on motion verbs and deadjectival verbs. In my PhD program, I aim to explore how manner and result are syntactically related to causation and scalar change, and examine how the encyclopaedic content in roots constrains their distribution and interaction with syntax.
